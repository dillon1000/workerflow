## Dashboard

The **dashboard** (`/dashboard`) is the default landing view after sign-in. Use it as a starting point to reach your workflows and recent activity, depending on how your deployment surfaces shortcuts.

## Workflows list

The **workflows** page (`/workflows`) lists workflows in your workspace. From here you can:

- **Create** a new workflow. The app assigns an identifier and opens or navigates you toward editing.
- **Open** an existing workflow to continue editing or to review metadata.
- Move between workflows without losing your session; each workflow has its own **draft** graph and **published** version (see [Designing workflows](/docs/designing-workflows)).

## Opening the editor

Workflows open in the **editor** at:

- `/workflows/:workflowId/editor` for a standard workflow.
- `/workflows/:parentWorkflowId/subworkflow/:subworkflowId/editor` when editing a **sub-workflow** attached to a parent.

Sub-workflows use a different trigger model (parent context only). The library and validation reflect that mode.

## Naming and organization

Use clear workflow names so teammates can find graphs in the list. Deeper folder or tag features, if present in your build, follow the same list and open pattern.
